Polymer Additive Manufacturing

Fact or Friction?

Kieran Nar

PhD researcher investigating the friction and wear properties of Additively Manufactured (LS) polymer components.


Research topics include:

- The comprehensive characterisation of Laser Sintered Polyamide-12 surfaces.

- Effect of post-build actions on the properties of parts produced by Laser Sintering.

- Understanding the transient regime behaviour of polymer Additively Manufactured components. br>
- Evaluating the friction and wear properties of Laser Sintered solid lubricant filled Polyamide-12 components.
